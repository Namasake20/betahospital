Our Lab Services

We offer all routine laboratory tests, blood donor services, specialized & referral laboratory tests. For all established laboratory tests, we do not refer our patients elsewhere but there are a few of the newer and rare tests which we refer to our collaborators in United Kingdom, Italy, South Africa and USA. To receive the best & professional laboratory services, clients need to come with a duly filled laboratory request form from their doctor. There are however a few tests that the clients can make a self request; Malaria test, Haemoglobin tests, Blood Grouping, Pregnancy test, HIV blood testing with mandatory counseling, Cholesterol, Prostate Antigen (PSA) test and Blood Sugar test. For all the other tests patients need a doctor who will not only order the test but also interpret the results and recommend treatment.
